We are Glitsch

We come from different backgrounds in tech, fashion design, and art, but we have one thing in common: we are fed up with the invasion of our personal data by powerful corporations and governments. We believe we have the right to control our data and decide who can access it and how. Our products are not only functional but also aesthetic and expressive. They reflect our values and our style. They are a statement of resistance and a challenge to the status quo. Join us and become part of the Glitsch movement.
